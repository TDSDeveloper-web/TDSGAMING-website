Slope

Game Description:

Slope is a fast-paced, endless runner game where you control a ball rolling down a steep slope. Your goal is to keep the ball on the track as it speeds up, while avoiding obstacles and staying clear of the edges. The track twists and turns, making it harder to survive the longer you go!

Controls:

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Move the ball left and right.
  • Avoid obstacles: Navigate past red blocks and other dangers.
  • Stay on track: The game gets faster the longer you play, so stay focused and react quickly!

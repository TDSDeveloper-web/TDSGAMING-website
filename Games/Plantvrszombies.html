Plants vs. Zombies

Storyline:

In *Plants vs. Zombies*, a horde of fun-loving zombies is about to invade your home, and the only defense is your arsenal of zombie-zapping plants! Use pea-shooting plants to defeat zombies before they can breach your front yard. Each wave of zombies gets stronger and more complex as they attempt to reach your house.

Game Controls:

Controls for PC:

  • Mouse: Click to select and place plants on the lawn grid.

Controls for Mobile:

  • Tap: Tap to select plants and place them strategically on the grid.

Choose from a wide variety of plants, each with unique abilities, to stop the zombies from advancing. Collect sun to grow your plants, and make sure to place your defenses wisely!

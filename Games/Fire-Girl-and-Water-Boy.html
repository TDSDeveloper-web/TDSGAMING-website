Fireboy and Watergirl in the Forest Temple

Storyline:

In *Fireboy and Watergirl in the Forest Temple*, the two characters must work together to overcome obstacles and solve puzzles in an ancient temple. Fireboy can handle fire but must avoid water, while Watergirl can handle water but must avoid fire. They need to collect gems and navigate through tricky levels filled with hazards, switches, and traps to reach their exits safely.

Controls:

Controls for PC:

  • Fireboy: Use the arrow keys to move.
  • Watergirl: Use the WASD keys to move.

Guide both characters simultaneously or team up with a friend for cooperative gameplay!

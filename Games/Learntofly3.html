Learn to Fly 3

Game Description:

Learn to Fly 3 is a fun physics-based game where you help a determined penguin fly into space! Use upgrades, purchase new equipment, and adjust your strategies to launch higher and farther with every attempt.

Controls:

PC:

  • Arrow keys: Adjust the angle during flight.
  • Spacebar: Use boosts.
  • Mouse: Navigate menus and upgrade items.
